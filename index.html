The PPL Values

People

‘People’ represent the heart of our practice as we built our business and brand around them. They are all the member of the society that we come in contact within our business and we act for their benefit and welfare.

Place

‘Place’ is the area that people use for specific purposes and activities and we are all about making these places enjoyable, safe and healthy for everyone.

Lifestyle

“Lifestyle” research is embedded into the heart of our practice as we built our business and brand around it.

Services

Design

Our design team champion ethical standards and construction, ensuring all projects are delivered with the welfare of the building users and construction works in mind.

Evelina Index

Algorithm with the ability to score a building based on the materials and construction embodied energy and ethical approach.

Evelina PO

Desktop post occupancy evaluation system able to analysis data collected from social media, giving a detailed overview on the building perception.

Education

CPD program addressing the importance of ethical values within the industry. Hosting lectures and seminars discussing these issues and our on-going research.
